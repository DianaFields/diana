IDENTITY

Project:

Graphic identity

Role:

Visual designer

Client:

Orange e-consulting

THE COMPANY

Orange is a Mexican consulting that provides solutions for B2B and B2C e-commerce projects. The company is formed by a group of youth talent specialized in CMS Magento.

THE SOLUTION

They asked me to design an identity that combines their IT background and an empathy approach methodology through a contemporary style. The identity is made by an icon that represents an orange but also can be seen as the turn-on icon. The chromatic selection was decided in order to be coherence with their main platform tool which is Magento.
