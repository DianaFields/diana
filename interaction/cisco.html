The concept

In many high schools students have limited access to experimentation in the lab due to safety concerns. Our proposal was create a virtual laboratory loaded with activities in order to allow students get the hands-on experience their school cannot provide.
TESTING METHODOLOGY Card sorting

Using the card sorting method we identified usersâ€™ expectations and categories that are important for them, in order to recognize opportunity areas. We gave the testers cards with our ideas on them, and ask them to organize and rate the cards, and to provide feedback.

THE TEAM

The team was conformed by product, interior and visual designers. We combine our expertice areas to generate a digital laboratory that allows students experiment with elements that are can be used in scholar environment.
