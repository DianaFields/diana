WHAT IS IRIS?

A system that change the way to access to your favorite media content

Iris collects and simplifies the information you care about most. Using an APP you can choose what content you wish to receive, including SMS, Whatsapp messages,emails, news, playlists, and media from your favorite applications. The Iris device stores your chosen information and plays the content as audio when you wish.

INSPIRED BY THE PAST

We looked at the intuitive interactions and simplicity of the radio set to inform the design and functionality of our product.

RESEARCH MARKET TRENDS
By 2020
10 devices per capita
30 billion connected devices
INSIGHTS

There is space in the market for an unobtrusive connected device that does not require a visual display, yet shares important information via audio experiences

AN ARDUINO PROJECT

Iris is running with Arduino YUN shield in order to works as a port to receive data from the cloud. Is provided by two potentiometers and 1 rotator sensor to control volume, channels and notifications. The system can be costumized to access data applications and this would be managed by the user throught an APP.

  • Arduino YUN
  • Sensor
  • Potentiometer
