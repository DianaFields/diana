DIGITAL PLATFORMS

Digital interfaces

Touchscreen interfaces for temperature control systems

Project:

Softermia S.A De C.V.

Role:

Web and user experience designer

THE COMPANY

Softermia is a mexican company that provides temperature and humidity management technology. I was in charge of designing all the digital interfaces for all their projects. Since the start point I collaborate in the user journey definition to the end by creating the user interface of the final product.

USER INTERFACE

The company develops control systems using touchscreen technology. They provide their clients costumed interfaces for all different performance and aesthetic needs. I designed a group of icons to be addapted to any of their devices resolution as well as all the experience to navigate through the interface.

WEB

As part of the positioning of the company in the Mexican market, they asked me to design a new website that reflects their values through a modern and simple graphic style. Part of my job was created wireframes, brand guidelines, user experience and user interface.
