Bitrubio Health Care

Project:

Bitrubio

Role:

UX/UI designer

Experience

Bitrubio is a platform to connect people and medical specialist.

The App

Through the app, patients would find specialist based on ratings and location, arrange medical appointments, consult doctors online, store health records and have feedback from other patients.

I was in charge of the UI/UX design for the mobile app and the website. My role was to generate the experience since the user journey until the creation of high fidelity prototypes.
